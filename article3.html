News Update: From the Tikau Emirate

Wednesday (1st Rajab 1446 - 01/01/2025)

HRH Alhaji Abubakar Muhammadu Ibn Grema II (Mai Tikau) has received a visit from barristers from Damaturu town. Those who visited His Royal Highness (Mai Tikau) at Tikau Palace in Sabon Nangere town were welcomed warmly.
