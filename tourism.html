GUJAM CAVE

It is a name of village where the cave is located, it has been in existence for 130 years. It is about 7km away from Nangere local government area by the eastern part and it contains two tribes which are the Kare-Kare and the Fulani. The Kare-Kare dominate 80% of the population while the Fulani dominate 20% of it. According to history, the Kare-Kare people are hunters and farmer. They discovered the cave while hunting and figured out that those are where hyenas and some other animals resided. They developed a unique relationship with the hyenas and other animals where has it does not hurt them rather it protects them from danger.
