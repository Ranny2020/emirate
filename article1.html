News Update: Makwi Majalam pay the Tikau Emirate a visit.

December 30, 2024

HRH Alhaji. Abubakar Muhammadu Ibn Grema II (Mai Tikau) received the visitor from Majalam. They went to pay their respect to his royal highness (Mai Tikau) HRH Alhaji. Abubakar Muhammadu Ibn Grema II at the palace in the new town of Nangere (sabon garin).
