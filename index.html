Present Emir of Tikau Emirate

ALHAJI ABUBAKAR MUHAMMADU IBN GREMA, The eldest of Alh. Dr. Muhammadu Abubakar Ibn Grema, the late Emir of Tikau. Alhaji Abubakar Muhammadu Ibn Grema has a Bachelor of Agric Economics & Extension, from Bayero University Kano,and MSc in Agric Economics from university of Readings, United Kingdom (Applied Econometrics, Development and Resource Economics) and a PhD from Yobe State University (inview) in Rural Development. He has worked in various capacities at Yobe state University. Before his appointment, Abubakar Muhammadu Ibn Gream, was the Deputy Director Agric Services, at Yobe State University and also the district Head of Sabon Gari and Yarima of Tikau Emirate.
